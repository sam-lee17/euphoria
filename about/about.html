ALL ABOUT US

The Euphoria

Greetings! This is Samantha Lee and Noela Chung from The Euphoria.
Due to the ongoing pandemic situation, we decided to take a different approach to connect with dancers around schools in Massachusetts. Our initial plan was to meet with the dancers once a month at a studio near the Brooks School to prepare for group performances. However, regarding the fact that there are students with remote learning status, we decided to start with a blog instead of performances to make steady progress under this circumstance. The blog will function as a platform for dancers to share their ideas on mental wellness. We will try our best to provide valuable ideas and experience based stories to help readers' mental wellness during this period of time along with great fundraisers!

OUR INTENTION

The Aim

These are some major themes that our club focuses on (you can click on each themes for further explanations):

  • Mental Wellness

    Our aim is to provide readers with experience based stories of how dancers can maintain their mental wellness. Taking into account that the pandemic situation is making a lot people aroudn the world depressing, we will try to serve people with ways to help each other and survive the ongoing pandemic.

  • Cooperation

    Our team includes leaders from each school in Massachusetts who are dancers from different backgrounds and experiences.

  • Inclusivity / Accpetance

    We will take into consideration how people have different perspectives and opinions. We will also make every effort to provide a judgment-free environment, where all members can feel accepted as who they are.

  • Contribution to society

    Our team will hold various kinds of fundraisers to donate to organizations and society groups with shortage due to the pandemic situation. First, we will begin with making a dance tee-shirt out of upucycling on left-over fabrics.k
