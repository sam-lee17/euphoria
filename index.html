Our Aim These are some major themes that our club focuses on:

Cooperation

Our team includes leaders from each school in Massachusetts who are dancers from different backgrounds and experiences.
Learn More

Mental Wellness

Our aim is to provide readers with experience based stories of how dancers can maintain their mental wellness. Taking into account that the pandemic situation is making a lot people aroudn the world depressing, we will try to serve people with ways to help each other and survive the ongoing pandemic.
Learn More

Inclusivity / Accpetance

We will take into consideration how people have different perspectives and opinions. We will also make every effort to provide a judgment-free environment, where all members can feel accepted as who they are.
Learn More

From Our Blog The blog will function as a platform for dancers to share their ideas on mental wellness. We will try our best to provide valuable ideas and experience based stories to help readers' mental wellness during this period of time along with great fundraisers!
